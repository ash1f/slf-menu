Who We
Are

Innovative

Logistics Experts

We are an innovative solutions provider for the logistics industry. At the heart of our services, lies Cargowise for which we are a business partner as well as a platinum service partner - the highest standard accredited to service partners.

This extends into our Connected Logistics Capabilities to provide the entire range of solutions spanning hardware and software. Our expertise spans process consulting, custom application development, integration solutions, design thinking and training.

Founded in 2010, we are fast-growing within the logistics industry with offices strategically located in the United States of America, Brazil, South Africa, India, Sri Lanka, Pakistan, Malaysia and New Zealand.

Led by a team of dedicated IT and logistics experts, SFLs performance driven approach will simplify and shape the future of logistics - Our expertise within the industry has enabled our customers to leverage & drive business growth.

At SFL, we believe in constantly challenging how things are done to create opportunities for us and our partners. Collaborate with us to stay ahead of the game.
